About me

Hello! Welcome to my portfolio. My name is Ricardo L. Garcia, 22 years old and I lived in Int. Edsa Balintawak, Unang Sigaw, Quezon City, Philippines. I graduated from EARIST - Eulogio "Amang" Rodriguez Institute of Science and Technology. I studied Bachelor of Science in Computer Science.   • Soft Skills: Creativity, Flexibility, Adaptability, Time Management, Teamwork, Problem Solving and Detail-Oriented
